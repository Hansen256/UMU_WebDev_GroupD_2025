About Web Space Uganda

Empowering businesses through innovative web solutions

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Web Space Uganda emerged from a simple vision: to make professional web development accessible to every Ugandan business, regardless of size or budget.

What started as a small team of three passionate developers has grown into a dynamic group of six professionals, each bringing unique skills and perspectives. We've worked with over 50 clients, from small startups to established enterprises, helping them establish and grow their online presence.

Today, we're proud to be a trusted partner for businesses across Uganda, delivering not just websites, but complete digital solutions that drive real results.
